**The Lever Under the World:** (This was written before 2023 and I'm not sure it still holds today.) We live in an age where we, as technologists, have the most individual power and leverage to move the world in the history of humanity. It's absolutely amazing to be alive right now. Where are all the people who are interested in working on our most challenging problems in construction, climate, government, etc.?
